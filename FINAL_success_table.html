üìä Key Recommendations for Entrepreneurs

1. Target Underserved Categories
Education (0.2% of tools, 25M+ views) and Business (<1% of tools, 29M+ views) offer the highest opportunity scores. Avoid development tools (87% saturated).

2. Optimize Content for Discovery
Include "AI" in your tool name/marketing (+41% visibility). Avoid generic "free" or "best" positioning which decreases performance by 47-62%.

3. Ride the Post-ChatGPT Wave
The market shifted permanently in Nov 2022. Post-ChatGPT content gets 3.2x more engagement. Position your tool in this new paradigm, not the old one.

4. Focus on Educational Value
Tutorial and "how-to" content performs 2.5x better than comparison/review content. Build tools that teach, not just tools that automate.
